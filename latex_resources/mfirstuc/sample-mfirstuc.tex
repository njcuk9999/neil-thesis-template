Formatting for the entire phrase must go outside
\verb|\capitalisewords| (unlike \verb|\makefirstuc|). Compare:

\capitalisewords{\textbf{a book of rhyme.}}

with:

\textbf{\capitalisewords{a book of rhyme.}}
